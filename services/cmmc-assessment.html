Our Process

How We Get You Assessment-Ready

A structured, milestone-driven engagement designed to compress your timeline and eliminate rework.

01

Scoping & CUI Flow Analysis

We identify exactly which systems, data flows, and personnel are in scope for your CMMC assessment. This starts with your DFARS 252.204-7012 contract clauses and ends with a documented CUI boundary — the single most important deliverable in the entire process. Bad scoping is the #1 cause of CMMC failures.

02

110-Control Gap Analysis

We assess your current implementation status against every NIST 800-171 Rev 2 control in scope. Each control gets a status (Implemented, Partially Implemented, Not Implemented, Not Applicable), supporting evidence notes, and a prioritized remediation recommendation. You'll receive a current SPRS score and a clear roadmap to close every gap.

03

Remediation Support

We don't just hand you a spreadsheet and walk away. We work alongside your IT team to implement the technical and procedural changes required — from access control policies and MFA deployment to audit log configuration and incident response procedures. Every remediation is documented with the evidence artifacts your C3PAO will require.

04

System Security Plan (SSP) Development

We build your complete SSP documenting how every in-scope control is implemented, the responsible parties, and the supporting technology stack. This is the foundational document your C3PAO will use as the basis for their assessment — it has to be accurate, complete, and aligned with your actual environment.

05

Evidence Package Assembly & Mock Assessment

We compile the complete evidence package — screenshots, policy documents, configuration exports, and interview scripts — organized by control family. Then we conduct a mock assessment simulating the C3PAO evaluation to identify any remaining gaps before your real assessment day.
