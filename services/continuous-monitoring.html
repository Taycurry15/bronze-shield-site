The Problem

Authorization Lapses Cost Contracts

Getting an ATO is hard. Keeping it is harder. Systems operating under Authorization to Operate require continuous monitoring — monthly vulnerability scans, POA&M updates, SCA findings adjudication, and periodic reassessment. When continuous monitoring lapses, authorization lapses. When authorization lapses, mission capability stops and contracts are at risk.

Most organizations treat the ISSM role as a part-time responsibility layered on top of someone's primary job. That works until a scan cycle surfaces 200 findings, half of which are false positives that need technical justification, and the rest need remediation plans with realistic timelines. Without dedicated ISSM attention, POA&Ms go stale, authorization status becomes a surprise, and the AO loses confidence in the program.

We provide the same continuous monitoring rigor we apply as ISSM of record on active DoD programs — because that is exactly what we have been doing for the past decade.

What We Do

Monthly ISSM-Level Operations

Scaled to your operational tempo — from full-time embedded support to periodic monthly engagements.

Vulnerability Assessment & Scan Analysis

Monthly ACAS (Tenable Nessus/Security Center), SCAP Compliance Checker (SCC), STIGViewer, and eMASS v3.x scan execution and analysis. Monthly deliverables include ACAS scan differential analysis, POA&M status updates formatted for eMASS bulk upload, updated hardware/software inventory reconciliation, and a ConMon status brief suitable for AO review. We document the technical justification for every finding disposition so your records withstand assessment scrutiny.

POA&M Lifecycle Management

Create, track, update, and close POA&M items with assigned owners, realistic milestones, and documented progress evidence. No stale items, no unassigned findings, no expired deadlines.

SCA Findings Management

Security Control Assessment findings from annual reviews require tracked remediation. We manage the findings lifecycle from initial identification through closure, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks between assessment cycles.

eMASS Artifact Maintenance

Keep your eMASS package current — SSP updates, control narrative revisions, artifact uploads, and configuration change documentation. Your package stays assessment-ready at all times.
