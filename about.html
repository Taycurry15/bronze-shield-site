About The Bronze Shield

Mission-Grade Cybersecurity,

The Bronze Shield is the cybersecurity consulting arm of Curry Solutions LLC — a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business delivering RMF, CMMC, and information security services to the defense industrial base.

Keontay Curry, CISM

CEO & Principal Consultant — The Bronze Shield
MSgt, Kentucky Air National Guard · Cyber Defense Operations

Keontay Curry is a service-disabled veteran and cybersecurity practitioner with over a decade of experience securing information systems across the Department of Defense. He has served as the Information System Security Manager (ISSM) of record on active DoD programs, managing the full RMF lifecycle from system categorization through Authority to Operate — including eMASS artifact development, continuous monitoring, and direct AO engagement.

His career spans multiple military branches and federal agencies: Navy PEO Digital, Army XCTC programs, Air Force cloud migration initiatives on Cloud One (AWS GovCloud at IL5), and private sector healthcare (Baptist Health System, where he served as Director of Cybersecurity and Disaster Recovery). He currently serves as a Master Sergeant in the Kentucky Air National Guard as a Cyber Defense Operator.

Keontay founded The Bronze Shield to bring practitioner-level RMF and CMMC expertise to small and mid-size defense contractors who need senior security leadership without the overhead of large consulting firms. Every engagement is led directly by him — not delegated to junior analysts.
